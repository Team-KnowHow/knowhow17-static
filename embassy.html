Country based questionnaire using Slides

Questionnaire based on the flags, monuments and photos on slides (30 slides), each slide will remain for 30 seconds and participants have to write the name of the related nation in a paper.

Crosswords and Cross-roads

Crosswords based on popular facts, stats and events of nations. And Cross- roads is scenario based written solutions question that will judge the creativity of the participant.

Model United Nations

Senate will control the discussion. Everyone will speak with reference to senate. Amongst the 10 finalists, 3 countries will have profile of Veto power. They will have to discuss on issues of Civil wars and WTO cases. For the first five minutes they will address the whole panel with their arguments and discussion will start after that according to their arguments. At last there will be voting on issues. Jury will act as the speaker of the house and three Seniors will act as the nations who have the Veto power so the direction of argument stays on track.
